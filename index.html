Getting there

Reachable from Tuguegarao and the rest of Cagayan Valley in a half-day drive.

MAP: Gonzaga, Cagayan
From Tuguegarao City

About 2.5–3 hours by van or private vehicle, via the Cagayan Valley Road heading north.

From Aparri

Around 1 hour east along the coastal road toward Gonzaga.

Landmark

Near the mouth of the Cagayan River — ask locals for Balai Baibai in Gonzaga if you lose signal.
